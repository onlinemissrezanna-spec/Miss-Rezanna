A Legacy of Elegance

Rooted in Heritage

MISS REZANNA was born out of a profound respect for the artistry of Indian textiles and a vision to redefine modern luxury. As the premier luxury brand born from the esteemed Kinshu Knitwear legacy, we carry forward decades of unparalleled expertise in fabric mastery and garment construction.

Guided by our founder, Pravel Setia, MISS REZANNA was established to bridge the gap between traditional, intricate Indian craftsmanship and the fluid, contemporary silhouettes demanded by the modern woman.

The Vision of Pravel Setia

Under the visionary leadership of Pravel Setia, MISS REZANNA operates on a simple yet powerful philosophy: clothing should amplify a woman's inherent grace without ever overshadowing it.

From our headquarters at Kinshu Knitwears in Ludhiana, Punjab, every collection is conceptualized with the dynamic, multifaceted lifestyle of our patrons in mind. We source the finest silks, breathable linens, and sheer organzas to ensure that every garment feels as luxurious as it looks.

"True luxury is not just in the aesthetic, but in the feeling of absolute confidence it provides the moment you put it on."

— Pravel Setia

Thoughtfully crafted for women who appreciate timeless elegance.
